Human Design. Machine Intelligence

State of the art machine learning models for fashion design, trend forecasting, and brand management. From trademark infringement detection to automated design portfolio development, and everything in between.

blender

blender is a tool for deriving stylistically similar items from a given collection, within a single product category (e.g. construct a "chunky sneaker" design from a set of inspirations).

seasons

seasons is a tool for constructing thematically similar items from a portfolio (e.g. given 4 items in Fall/Winter 2019, design 6 items in different categories to fill out the portfolio).

distinct

distinct is a tool for staying ahead of trends, by designing items that look nothing like what is currently on the market.

antitrademark

antitrademark is a tool for monitoring images (e.g. on social media, or from a given portfolio) to ensure brand-specific design elements are not included (or the inverse, for advertising).
